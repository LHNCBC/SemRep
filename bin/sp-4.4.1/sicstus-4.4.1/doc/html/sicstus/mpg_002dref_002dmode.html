11.3.123 mode/1   declaration

Synopsis

:- mode +Mode

Currently a dummy declaration.

Arguments

:Mode

term

Exceptions

Exceptions in the context of loading code are printed as error messages.

context_error

Declaration appeared in a goal.

permission_error

Declaration appeared as a clause.
