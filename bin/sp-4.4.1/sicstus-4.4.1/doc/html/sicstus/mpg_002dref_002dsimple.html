11.3.209 simple/1

Synopsis

simple(+Term)

Term is currently not instantiated to a compound term.

Arguments

Term

term

Examples

| ?- simple(9).

yes
| ?- simple(_X).

yes
| ?- simple("a").

no

Exceptions

None.
