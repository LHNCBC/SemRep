11.3.205 set_output/1   ISO

Synopsis

set_output(+Stream)

makes Stream the current output stream.

Arguments

Stream

stream_object, must be ground

A valid output stream.

Exceptions

Stream errors (see ref-iou-sfh-est).

See Also

tell/1, ref-iou-sfh.
