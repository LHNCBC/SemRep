2 Platforms

Binary distributions of Release 4.4 are available for many platforms; see https://sicstus.sics.se/ for an up-to-date list.

SICStus has, at one time or another, been ported to many platforms, ranging from mobile phones to mainframes. If your platform is not currently listed on the download page, then please let us know (sicstus-request@sics.se).
